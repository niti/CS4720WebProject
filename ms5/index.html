ABOUT

charlottesville, virginia is located in center of the commonwealth of virginia, home to the university of virgina in a city immeresed with traditions with a southern charm and plenty of good food.

the purpose of this website is to provide a central location to keep track of all the resturants, vineyards, cafes etc. around the university's historic grounds to downtown charlottesville for students, visitors, and locals. it will allow for users to keep track of new specials, resturant openings, customer reviews for many vendors around c-ville. in all, it will serve as a tracking of all things food related in cville!

Resturants

Restaurants from UVa's corner such as The Virginia to Boylan Heights to Downtown Charlottesville very finest Citizen Burger will be explored.

Vineyards

Charlottesville surrounds itself near the Blue Ridge Mountains with a number of wonderful vineyards taking residence to offer a great afternoon outing to wedding spots around Charlottesville.

Cafes

Coffee lovers all unite to Charlottesville. Reviews from all great cafes such as MudHouse downtown to Para coffee on the Uva Corner will help you find that very special coffee house for you.
